Moving Tips & Advice

Discover expert advice to make your move in Canberra, Sydney, or interstate as smooth as possible. Our tips are designed to save you time, reduce stress, and ensure your belongings are handled with care.

How to Pack Fragile Items

Use high-quality packing materials like bubble wrap, packing paper, and sturdy boxes. Wrap each fragile item individually and fill empty spaces in boxes with cushioning materials to prevent shifting. Clearly label boxes as "Fragile" and ensure they are securely taped.

Moving to Canberra: A Guide

Canberra offers a relaxed lifestyle with excellent amenities. Research suburbs like Belconnen for family-friendly areas or Braddon for a vibrant city vibe. Plan your move during off-peak seasons to save costs and avoid traffic congestion.

Decluttering Before Your Move

Sort your belongings into four categories: keep, donate, sell, or discard. Decluttering reduces moving costs and makes unpacking easier. Donate usable items to local charities in Canberra or Sydney, and sell valuable items online to offset moving expenses.

Interstate Moving Tips

When moving between Canberra and Sydney, hire a removalist experienced in interstate moves. Create a detailed inventory of your items and ensure they are insured. Pack a separate bag with essentials like toiletries, chargers, and snacks for easy access during transit.

Moving with Pets

Keep your pets calm by maintaining their routine. Use a secure pet carrier for transportation and pack their essentials, including food, water, and toys. Update their microchip details with your new address and consult your vet for travel tips if needed.

Using Storage Solutions

If your new home isn’t ready, consider short-term storage options. Opt for climate-controlled facilities to protect sensitive items like furniture and electronics. Label boxes clearly and create an inventory for easy retrieval when needed.

Budget-Friendly Moving Tips

Save money by packing yourself and sourcing free boxes from local stores. Compare quotes from multiple removalists and book early to secure the best rates. Avoid moving during peak times like weekends or holidays to reduce costs.

Preparing for a Same-Day Move

Pack an essentials box with items like toiletries, chargers, snacks, and a change of clothes. Start early and enlist help from friends or family. Hire a reliable removalist to ensure everything is completed on time and without stress.

Checklist for Moving Day

Prepare a checklist to ensure nothing is forgotten. Include tasks like confirming the removalist’s arrival time, packing a first-aid kit, and ensuring utilities are set up at your new home. Double-check all rooms before leaving.

Eco-Friendly Moving Tips

Reduce waste by using reusable packing materials like plastic bins. Donate or recycle unwanted items responsibly. Choose a removalist that uses fuel-efficient vehicles to minimize your carbon footprint.

Handling Heavy Furniture

Disassemble large furniture to make it easier to move. Use furniture sliders or dollies to prevent injuries and protect floors. Always lift with your legs, not your back, and enlist help for heavy items.

Tips for Moving in Bad Weather

Protect your belongings with waterproof covers and tarps. Wear non-slip shoes and keep pathways clear of mud or snow. Communicate with your removalist to adjust plans if necessary.
